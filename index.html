Crocodile Coding

What We Do

Crocodile Coding is a tutoring organization founded by four high schoolers from the Bay Area. We are dedicated to teaching children coding and inspiring them to pursue programming as a passion. We currently start with 4th to 8th grade students who are seeking to learn the rudiments of computer science in Scratch, and, in the future, we will offer various courses about further advanced concepts and languages such as Python and JavaScript. We hope to introduce children to the marvels of computer science and kindle their enthusiasm for its applications.

Online Classes

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, we use Zoom to teach our students virtually while maintaining the safety of the community.

Tailor-made Curriculum

Our course material is carefully crafted to build a strong foundation, creating reusable skills that will give your young coders the ability to go far in their endeavours.

Experienced Instructors

All of our instructors have years of programming experience, ensuring your child is being taught by those well-versed in the world of computer science.

Courses

Scratch

Learn Scratch, a basic yet flexible visual programming language developed by MIT for beginners. This class is an excellent introduction to the world of programmming, and one that's sure to interest your young student.
