Crocodile Coding

What We Do

Crocodile Coding is a tutoring organization founded by four high schoolers from the Bay Area. We are dedicated to teaching children coding and inspiring them to pursue programming as a passion. We currently start with 4th to 8th grade students who are seeking to learn the rudiments of computer science, and, in the future, we will offer courses about further advanced concepts and languages such as Python and JavaScript. Our mission is to introduce children to the marvels of computer science and kindle their enthusiasm for its applications.

Online Classes

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, we use Google Meets to teach our students virtually and to maintain the safety of our community.

Customized Curriculum

Our course material is carefully crafted to build a strong foundation, developing skills that will give young coders the ability to go far in their future endeavours.

Experienced Instructors

All of our instructors have years of programming experience, ensuring your child is being taught by those well-versed in the world of computer science.

Courses

Scratch

Learn Scratch, a basic yet flexible visual programming language developed by MIT for beginners. This class is an excellent introduction to the world of programming, and one that's sure to interest young students.

Curriculum

  1. What is Coding?
  2. Sprites and Backdrops
  3. Movement and Position
  4. Variables
  5. Math Operators
  6. Boolean Operators
  7. Conditionals
  8. Broadcasting and Sensing
  9. Loops
  10. More Math Operators
  11. Functions and Parameters
  12. Sprite Words and Word Operators
  13. Clones
  14. Lists

Python

Learn Python, a typed programming language that is easy to learn for beginners with its English-like syntax. This course covers foundational programming concepts for late-elementary and middle school aged students and is will be able to act as a stepping stone for future coding endeavors.
